About @ferdbot

Ferdbot (Chip Fasciana) is an artist who lives and works in Kailua, Hawaii. He has painted a wide variety of public murals in New York State and California. He is a painter & Muralist, Sculptor, Designer and Curator. He works almost exclusively with Upcycled/Reclaimed materials. Animals and their symbolism are often the subject of his work.

He has shown his work extensively in New York City, Southern California as well as London UK. He recently painted a large mural in Kailua. For years he organized and Curated many successful Underground Pop-Up Art Shows in vacant buildings in New York.

He has won a variety of awards including Artist of the Year from Albany Center Galleries as well as Masterpiece Award from the Albany Institute of History and Art. His artwork has been featured in The New York Times.
